Highlands Voices

We are a nationally ranked a cappella choir from Northern Highlands in Allendale, NJ.

ABOUT US

Music Production

Highlands Voices is releasing their very first CD this month. The title of the CD is Decades and it includes music from every decade since their school has been established.

ICHSA Champions

Last choir season, Voices took home the ICHSA Mid-Atlantic Regional Title in the semi-finals. Highlands has also been recognized for Outstanding Soloists and Choreography.
