About Us

  • Highlands Voices




    The Highlands Voices are from Northern Highlands Regional High School in Allendale, NJ. The “Voices” as they are called around their school, have been ICHSA Mid-Atlantic A Cappella Champions for the past three years (2011, 2012, 2013). The group is directed by Thomas Paster and is equally comfortable singing classical, vocal jazz or contemporary a cappella. They have performed in Avery Fischer Hall, Alice Tully Hall, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, as well as festivals, concerts, and competitions around the country. Numerous group members have participated in Bergen County Chorus, Region 1 Chorus, NJ All-State Chorus, and All-National Chorus. They are about to release their first contemporary a cappella CD titled Decades, which spans six decades of popular music.

  • Jamie Tofano

    My name is Jamie Tofano, and I am a 17 year old senior at Northern Highlands Regional High School. I have loved music for as long as I can remember. I started singing at a very early age, picked up classical piano at the age of four, and now write music on my own. I was part of our schools all-girl a cappella group “Belles”, and it is now my third year being a member of the Highlands Voices where I sing as an Alto 2, and occasionally step in as vocal percussionist. I won outstanding soloist alongside Tricia Wilson in last year’s ICHSA competition, and derive great joy in being part of such an outstanding group. Personally, I am outgoing, loud, funny, and a silly individual and I can often be found cracking jokes. I’m grateful that I have been able to find an outlet like the Highlands Voices, and in this last year with the friends I’ve made, I hope we accomplish an immense amount and grow even closer than we already are.
